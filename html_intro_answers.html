"Kimi no Na wa." (Your Name)

"Kimi no Na wa." (Your Name) is a critically acclaimed anime movie directed by Makoto Shinkai and released in 2016. It blends romance, fantasy, and drama with breathtaking animation and an emotional storyline.

The story follows Mitsuha Miyamizu, a high school girl from a rural town, and Taki Tachibana, a boy from Tokyo, who mysteriously begin swapping bodies. As they navigate each other’s lives, they form a deep connection. However, just as they start to understand their strange bond, an unexpected twist threatens to separate them forever.

Main Characters

  1. Taki Tachibana

    • A high school boy living in Tokyo.
    • Passionate about architecture and works part-time at a restaurant.
    • Initially confused by the body-swapping but grows attached to Mitsuha.
    • Determined, caring, and willing to go to great lengths to find her.

  2. Mitsuha Miyamizu

    • A high school girl from the rural town of Itomori.
    • Feels trapped by her small-town life and shrine maiden duties.
    • Mysteriously starts swapping bodies with Taki.
    • Kind, strong-willed, and longs for something beyond her hometown.
